About KB-Infinity- Lab

KB-Infinity- Lab (Kigali Intelligent Robotics & AI Laboratory) is an applied research lab focused on edge AI, intelligent control, and safe autonomous systems. We bridge advanced academic research with deployable real-world robotics.

Research Pillars

Edge AI & Perception

Vision transformers, uncertainty modeling, efficient inference.

Intelligent Control

Hybrid MPC + learning, robust and safe autonomy.

Robotics Systems

Mobile robots, sensor fusion, embedded platforms.

Safety & Monitoring

Anomaly detection in cyber-physical systems.

Flagship Projects

URBAN-NAV

Uncertainty-aware vision-based autonomous navigation using hybrid MPC on edge robotics platforms.

SAFE-EDGE

AI-driven anomaly detection and safety monitoring for autonomous and cyber-physical systems.
